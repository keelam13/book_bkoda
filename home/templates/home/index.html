BKODA Seat Reservation

Welcome to BKODA Seat Reservation, your easy-to-use online platform for booking trips between Kabayan, Benguet, and Baguio City! Developed specifically for the BKODA (Benguet-Kabayan Operators and Drivers Association), our goal is to simplify your travel plans by allowing you to conveniently reserve seats from anywhere.

Whether you're a regular commuter or planning a one-time trip, our application is designed to provide a seamless experience. You can quickly search for available routes and schedules, view real-time seat availability, and secure your booking with just a few clicks. For registered users, managing your existing reservations, including modifications, is straightforward and hassle-free.

Why Choose BKODA Seat Reservation?

  • Effortless Booking: Find and reserve seats for your desired trips with ease.
  • Convenience: Book anytime, anywhere, without needing to visit a physical terminal.
  • Trip Management: Registered users can view and manage their reservations directly through the app.
  • Local Focus: Tailored to the travel needs of the Kabayan, Benguet, and Baguio City routes, understanding the community's specific requirements.

Our Vision

We understand that for many in our community, cash payments are the norm. While our current focus is on providing a robust and reliable reservation system, we are actively exploring future enhancements, including diverse payment options, to further improve your experience.

Feel free to explore the app and make your next trip reservation today!
